GeM Platform Redesign

Dashboard & CMS System Design

Overview

The Government e-Marketplace (GeM) required a comprehensive redesign of its CMS and dashboard systems to improve usability for thousands of vendors and government buyers. The goal was to simplify complex data visualization and streamline the procurement workflow.

My Role

As the lead UI/UX Designer, I was responsible for end-to-end design execution. This included stakeholder interviews, creating wireframes, establishing a new design system, and delivering high-fidelity prototypes for the engineering team.

Key Challenges

Balancing the display of dense data with an approachable interface was the primary challenge. We needed to ensure that users with varying levels of technical proficiency could easily navigate the platform without feeling overwhelmed.

Results

The redesign led to a 40% reduction in support tickets related to navigation and a significant improvement in task completion rates among newly onboarded vendors.
